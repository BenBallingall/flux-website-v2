Slack

We use Slack to communicate; it's a feature rich chat app for teams with a web client as well as apps for most devices.

We currently maintain two Slack teams.

  • The first is a publicly accessible Slack team for members at fluxvolunteers.slack.com. However, to access this you'll need to invite yourself at https://slack.voteflux.org/. When we mention slack in regards to meetings, this is the Slack team we're referring to.
  • The second is a closed Slack team for administrative business, and is mostly a convenience to avoid using private channels on the volunteers Slack, which quickly becomes cumbersome.
