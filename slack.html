Slack

We use Slack to communicate; it's a feature rich chat app for teams with a web client as well as apps for most devices.

Our Slack team for members and volunteers is at fluxvolunteers.slack.com, but you'll need to have an account first. You can send yourself a signup link by heading to https://slack.voteflux.org/ and using your email address.
